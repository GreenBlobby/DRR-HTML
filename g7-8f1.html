Slippery Floors

The tiles look damp and dirty a lot of the time. When floors are wet or grimy, it’s easy to slip and fall – and if we’re carrying books or supplies, we could get even more hurt.

Exposed Wires and Parts

The cover is missing, so the wires and inside of the box are out in the open. If we accidentally touch them – maybe while walking by, reaching for something nearby, or playing around – we could get a bad electric shock.

Not Safe For Those Who Need It Most

PWD students rely on this area to move around comfortably. The missing light source (light bulb) poses a significant risk of more accidents as the lack of visibility makes it harder to navigate.
